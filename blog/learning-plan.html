Learning Plan

09 January 2024

1. Long-Term Goal

My long-term goal is to become a confident and skilled coder, ultimately securing a job in the tech field.

2. Strengths and Limitations

I am dedicated to learning, demonstrating commitment and perseverance when pursuing my goals. Once I decide on a course of action, I stay focused and dedicated.

On the limitations note, when I fail, I easily lose motivation, and it takes me a while to get over it.

3. Personal Development Goals

I would like to develop more habits, communication confidence - enhance my ability to express ideas clearly and confidently. Learn effective collaboration within a team, understanding roles and responsibilities. Develop strong time management skills to handle workload efficiently.

4. Workload Management and Coping with Pressure

I will proactively communicate with team members, facilitators, and industry representatives. I will regularly assess progress and adjust schedules as needed to meet deadlines. I will prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance.

I will regularly practice mindfulness techniques to manage stress. I will take short breaks to refresh when feeling overwhelmed. I will utilize support networks, such as peers and mentors, for guidance.

5. Seeking Help

I will promptly reach out to facilitators or mentors when facing challenges. I will engage in peer-to-peer learning, forming study groups to share insights. I will attend scheduled support sessions and workshops.

6. Expectations from the Facilitation Team

I expect clear communication of expectations and learning objectives. I seek constructive feedback and guidance on both technical and non-technical aspects. I anticipate availability for one-on-one discussions and support when needed.

7. Communication and Commitments

I will communicate in advance if any unforeseen commitments arise. While fully committed to dedicating the majority of my time to study, I acknowledge the potential for unexpected family commitments. During the foundation period, I plan to handle school drop-offs (8:30 to 9:00 am) and pick-ups (2:30 to 3:00 pm), assuring you that I am committed to managing these responsibilities efficiently to ensure minimal impact on my learning journey.
